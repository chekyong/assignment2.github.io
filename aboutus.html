Coach Chek Yong

The Train Station was established in 2018 by Coach Chek Yong who is an avid Kettlebell Sports enthusiast, coach and athlete and who has helped advocate kettlebell training across Asia.



Since then he was inspired to pass on the highest level of kettlebell sports competency from her Honorary Master of Sports International Class Coach Sergey Merkulin, a 16 times world champion, on to future kettlebell lovers.
